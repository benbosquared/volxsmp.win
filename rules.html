The Law of the Land

Server Rules

Break these and face the consequences. No exceptions, no excuses.

Punishments range from warnings and temporary mutes/bans to permanent bans at staff discretion.
Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.

🗣️ General Conduct
01Respect all players. Harassment, bullying, and targeted hate will not be tolerated. Trash talk is fine — personal attacks are not.
02No discrimination. Slurs or hate speech based on race, gender, religion, sexuality, or nationality will result in an immediate ban.
03No spam or excessive caps in chat. Keep it readable.
04No advertising other servers or discords in chat.
05English only in global chat. Use DMs for other languages.
⚙️ Gameplay Rules
06No hacking or cheating. This includes X-ray, kill aura, fly hacks, or any unfair advantage. Banned clients = permanent ban, no appeal.
07No exploiting bugs or glitches. Report them to staff — do not abuse them. Duping, item glitches, and map exploits are all included.
08No AFK machines or auto-clickers to farm resources unattended.
09No griefing spawn or protected areas. Player bases in the wild are fair game.
10No lag machines. Builds intentionally designed to lag the server will be removed and the player banned.
🏦 Economy & Trading
11No scamming in trades. Agree on terms before trading — staff will not refund items from deals gone wrong without evidence.
12No real-money trades (RMT) for in-game items outside of the official store.
👮 Staff & Appeals
13Respect staff decisions. If you disagree, appeal in Discord — don't argue in-game.
14No impersonating staff or claiming fake authority over other players.
15Ban appeals must be submitted in Discord. In-game begging will not be considered.

Questions or Appeals?

Join our Discord to reach staff, submit ban appeals, or report players.
